# Business Objectives

> LawMadeSimple: From legal jargon to legal empowerment.

---

## Vision Statement

**Every Nigerian understands the laws that affect their daily life.**

---

## Mission Statement

We translate complex Nigerian laws into plain language with practical examples, enabling citizens and SMEs to understand their rights and obligations without needing a law degree.

---

## Business Goals

| Goal | Description | Success Metric | Target (Year 1) |
|------|-------------|----------------|-----------------|
| **User Acquisition** | Build a user base of Nigerians seeking legal understanding | Monthly Active Users (MAU) | 10,000 MAU |
| **Comprehension Impact** | Users successfully understand laws through our explanations | "This was helpful" rating | 80%+ positive |
| **Content Coverage** | Cover the most common legal scenarios for target users | Laws covered with explanations | 6 core laws, 100+ scenarios |
| **Platform Trust** | Establish credibility through accuracy and transparency | Return user rate | 40%+ returning users |
| **Sustainability** | Build toward a sustainable business model | Operating costs covered | Break-even by Month 18 |

---

## Strategic Alignment

**Market Position:** First-mover in the "law comprehension" space for Nigeria

**Competitive Strategy:**
- Differentiate on *understanding*, not just *access*
- Target underserved market (non-lawyers) that competitors ignore
- Build moat through user-generated scenario data
- Position for future expansion (state laws, case law, languages)

**Solo Developer Strategy:**
- Leverage AI for content generation (explanations, examples)
- Use managed services to minimize ops burden
- Focus on core differentiator, outsource/defer everything else
- Quality over quantity â€” fewer laws, better explanations
